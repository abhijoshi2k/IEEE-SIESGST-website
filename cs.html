IEEE SIESGST CS Chapter

The Computer Society of IEEE SIESGST was launched on 2nd March, 2021. One of the main aims of the chapter is to develop strong support for students and keenness in them to take part in competitive coding programs. The chapter will help students explore fields such as Data Science, Machine Learning and Deep Learning from scratch.

Our Goal

“Our goal is to develop and inculcate computing skills in all students as well as to develop keenness in them to take part in various competitive coding programs.”
